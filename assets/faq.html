Q1：How could I know whether the socket connected with the router?

A1：If the socket connected with the router, the backlight of the on-off key will keep on.

Q2：Why can't connect the socket with the router?

A2：There may be several reasons：

1.  Please check if the other devices could access internet through your router?

2.  Maybe the network is disconnected in the configuration process, please try again.

3.  Maybe the inputted password is wrong, please check the inputted password.

4.  Please check the security configuration mode of your router, WPA/WPA2 is recommended.

4.  Please check the network mode, support 11b only，11g only，11bg mixed，11bgn mixed, not support 11n only.

5.  Maybe the connection numbers of your router exceed maximum limit, please close the temporary unused equipment, and try again.

 

Q3：If I changed the router, how could I connect the socket with the changed router?

A3：Connect your smartphone with the changed router first, and then configure WiFi smart socket according to the configuration method.

Q4：How could I control the socket after configured successfully?

A4：The socket will appear in 【Devices】 interface after configured successfully, the other users can enter 【Settings】 interface and click 【Search socket】 in 【Smart Setup】interface, if socket found, you can control socket in 【Devices】 interface.

Q5：Can I only control the socket in home?

A5：You can control socket not only in local area, but also in remote area, if the socket is online and your smartphone connected with internet, you can control the socket anywhere.

Q6：Why the socket is offline sometimes?

A6：Maybe the WiFi network is disconnected or unavailable, please check network connection or restart the router.

Q7：What kinds of home appliances the socket suits for?

A7：The socket suits for the home appliances whose power are no more than 10A and 2000W and which can power on and power down directly.

Q8：Is the on/off state of socket shown on the client real?

A8：The client support the real-time and bidirectional feedback of the socket's on/off state, so you can make the running status of your home appliances in your control.

Q9：Can I control multiple sockets?

A9：One client can control multiple sockets, the maximum number is 150.

Q10：If there are many sockets in my home, how could I find the socket that I want to open/close?

A10：You can set sockets info in 【Customise】 interface, such as socket name, socket icon, and then it's convenient for you to find the socket you want to open/close.

Q11：Whether other smartphone with the same client can control my socket from remote area?

A11：The socket can be found only in the same network, other people who don't know the password of your WiFi network can't find your socket. In addition, the socket support lock function which can prevent other people from finding and controlling your socket, and you can lock the socket in 【Customise】 interface after you and your family successfully added the socket.

Q12：What is【Modify Remote Password】 used for?

A12：Other people can't control your socket from remote area after the remote password modified. For example, if your friends come to your home, you install client on their smartphones to experience the socket, and you can modify remote password after they leave your home, which can prevent them from controlling your socket from remote area just for a joke.

Q13：How many timings can I set?

A13：The maximum timings you can set is 10, you can set the opening/closing time according to your living habit. For example, you can have a hot shower after getting up if you set one hour before you get up to open electric water heater.

Q14：What is countdown used for？

A14：It's used for closing or opening your home appliances after amount of time which is set according to your need. For example, if you charge your smartphone when you sleep at night, you can set three hours later to close the socket, so you don't have to worry about the explosion problem caused by too long charging time.
